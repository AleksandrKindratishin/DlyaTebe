About the Foundation

  • The All-Ukrainian Charitable Foundation "For You" was established in 2018 to provide assistance to children with illnesses from birth, hospitals, institutions, children deprived of parental care, and needy populations. The main focus of the foundation's work is the treatment of severely ill newborns. Our main goal is a healthy nation. Our work is aimed at reducing the percentage of child mortality and the development of pathological conditions and childhood disabilities in Ukraine. We support the work of rehabilitation centers and orphanages for children with disabilities.

  • We provide hospitals with the necessary equipment and materials for the treatment of young Ukrainians, as well as support departments and medical staff with the necessary resources for effective work and care for our wards. We work closely with medical institutions to provide maximum assistance in treating severely wounded military personnel.

  • The foundation also works to help the Armed Forces of Ukraine by sending humanitarian aid to the front line. Our team makes every effort to support shelters for Mothers and Children, and victims of hostilities who are forced to relocate, as well as lonely pensioners, with humanitarian aid. Ukrainian organizations and volunteers, as well as international foundations and associations, join our missions.

We believe in a healthy and happy future for our country!
Sincere gratitude to everyone who is with us!

Our Projects

Charity Basket

This is one of our initiatives that allows us to provide assistance to those in need in our country on a regular basis, including food, hygiene products, clothing, medicine, and more. Thanks to this project, orphaned children, large and low-income families, lonely pensioners, shelters, crisis centers, and forced migrants have the opportunity to receive necessary support during difficult times in their lives. We work closely with sponsors and foreign volunteers who support the foundation's project and send aid that is distributed to those in need in Ukraine. Such help is always relevant, especially during times of war on our land. We invite everyone who cares to join this charitable cause. Together we can achieve more!

Life for Heroes

Everyone wishes to have support, especially when it concerns our lives directly! Heroes of our time, who defend Ukrainian land, save the Great Nation, protect us and our children - now more than ever need our attention and support! The soldiers of our country pay a high price - their health and even their lives to give us a free future. They give us hope! Therefore, together we must go through this difficult path to Victory with them and provide necessary assistance to those who defend us all! Working on this project, our foundation considers it necessary to: - Support hospitals and departments where injured and sick military personnel are located with necessary medicines, drugs, and equipment. - Support our defenders on the front lines with humanitarian aid. We call on all caring people to join this project!

Tiny One, Live

it's about life! It's about the health of our nation. By saving newborn babies, we with you are building a bright and happy future for Ukrainians. Timely assistance in the treatment of newborns is crucial! This gives a great chance for a full and healthy future for every child who needs treatment from birth. Thanks to our support, hundreds of babies grow up healthy, even those born with developmental defects! With the help of doctors, we were even able to save a baby born weighing only 640g! What kind of support does our foundation provide under this project: - Support for premature babies, children after difficult births, with developmental defects, in intensive care units with medicines, medical supplies, payment for laboratory services, etc; - Ongoing support for sick babies who are left without parents with children's food, medicines, consumables, hygiene and care products, toys, clothes, and everything necessary for their health and normal development. Many children find their loving families..

Between Us

This internet project is aimed at interacting with the community, volunteers, donors, organizations, and experts from various fields of our country's activities. The project is created to provide informational assistance to the citizens of Ukraine. We will strive to cover the most important topics for people, relying on our experience and the expertise of professionals. By coming together, we will shape a healthy and conscious society. We invite professionals to join our project who are ready to provide informational assistance to people in the fields of medicine, psychology, law, and other helping professions.

Heartstrings

project was created as a creative music direction that brings together performances by musicians and various musical and creative groups with the aim of helping seriously ill newborn children recover! The main idea of this project is to create concerts and organize various genre musical performances at creative charity events that unite and involve compassionate viewers in supporting hospitals and intensive care units where seriously ill children are being treated. Together with musicians and creative groups from different countries around the world, we support children's hospitals in Dnipro. Funds raised at charity concerts and events are directed towards the treatment of children and the needs of pediatric departments. Currently, we are eager to expand our circle of creative friends who are willing to collaborate with our foundation to save new lives! Therefore, we invite music groups, vocalists, musicians, and simply compassionate people to join our "Heartstrings" team! We will be very happy to welcome new open hearts!
